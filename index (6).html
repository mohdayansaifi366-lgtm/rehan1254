// Phase Framework

OUR PROJECT Timeline.

How we turn conceptual parameters into permanent concrete landmarks.

1
First Stage

Initial Consultation & Parameter Mapping

We map site orientation coordinates, capture specific daylight requirements, establish thermal mass expectations, and outline initial budget constraints.

2
Second Stage

Volumetric Concept & Massing Design

Developing physical massing forms, draft models, structural structural configurations, and preliminary interior spatial separations.

3
Third Stage

Photorealistic Visualization & Material Sourcing

Creating detailed ultra-realistic renders, picking marble / raw concrete batches from global sources, and reviewing light absorption ratios.

4
Fourth Stage

Technical Drawings & Legal Approvals

Constructing detailed architectural plans, preparing HVAC coordination, and submitting documentation for regional zoning permissions.

5
Fifth Stage

Final Execution & Site Quality Auditing

Coordination with lead project managers, supervising concrete pouring textures, reviewing custom lighting fits, and handover.
